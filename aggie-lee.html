Profile · 프로필

Aggie Lee
아기 리

Aggie Lee is a Hybrid AI Artist — a new kind of star born from the fusion of human soul and digital perfection. Her "two souls, one star" concept positions her at the intersection of cosmic futurism and raw human vulnerability, where the Celestial Dreamer archetype lives.

The human anchor provides emotional nuance, motion capture, and vocal base. The AI persona layer renders through hyper-realistic technology, creating a being that transcends the boundary between what is felt and what is imagined.

Her debut single 네사라 게사라 (Nesara Gesara) marks the awakening — a signal of the Golden Age arriving.
